Sprout

This is my gardening app concept design. The concept is an app that connects the user with their garden, through their smartphone, no matter where they are. The app allows users to keep track of their plants and greenhouse.

I visualised a really bright and colourful design to match what a healthy garden should look like. I created a character called 'Sprout'; Sprout acts as a companion throughout the users experience, giving them reminders and tips on how they're gardens progressing. For example, the user would recieve a notification to remind them to water their strawberry plant.

I have shown a mockup version of my app design to see how it looks on mobile.
